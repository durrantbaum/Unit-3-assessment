My Neighborhood

My neighborhood is in Lehi, UT and I really enjoy living here! I like how it's basically right in between SLC and Provo. This is convenient for me because I have famliy in Provo and SLC, plus there are two big airports in those two cities that I use for travel. Lehi is a pretty quiet city and living in my suburban neighborhood feels relaxing and safe.
